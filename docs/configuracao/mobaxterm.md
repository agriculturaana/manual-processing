## Configuracao Ferramenta Opcional

### Clique em "New session"

![N|Solid](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/d/11NfwvA10erhrw_fPSDseaB-G4OfBKTSP)
### Marque a opção "SSH" :

![N|Solid](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/d/1dNXBz9eZ6twu6O0E2Ceku7IfQzWBDEz6)
### Informe o IP, Usuario e Porta conforme a imagem:

![N|Solid](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/d/1P7ghwmVV6NdrLCwwyyrndOEGXo4XrvRj)
### Clique na opção "Advanced SSH settings" marque a opção "Use private key" e selecione a chave da aws:

![N|Solid](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/d/1Tllf4kxRFUA2WVtk0DyPbs98dBHK1wwr)
### Selecione a chave ssh no diretorio da sua maquina:

![N|Solid](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/d/1s1gnJcXrSgu0wf_G56zTepoQGHZvS--b)
### Clique em "OK":

![N|Solid](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/d/1J5OiKpkUr6mDJJ59i5Gg-Yib_YlRkVIN)
### Pronto, está conectado:

![N|Solid](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/d/1L4krR1k4-bYi29yFcKbhqGuk-SP6bI-N)
